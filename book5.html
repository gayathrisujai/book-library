Heart Bones

by Colleen Hoover

Published: August 19, 2020
Genre: · Romance · Contemporary · Fiction
Pages: 338

*"Heart Bones"* by Colleen Hoover is a poignant novel that explores themes of love, loss, and resilience. The story follows Beyah Grim, a young woman from a troubled background who is determined to escape her harsh reality and start a new life. When she unexpectedly spends the summer with her estranged father in a small Texas town, she meets Samson, a mysterious boy with his own secrets. As Beyah and Samson grow closer, they discover that they have more in common than they initially thought, and together, they navigate the complexities of their pasts while forming a deep connection. The novel beautifully captures the struggle between hope and despair, highlighting the healing power of love.
