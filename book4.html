Ugly Love

by Colleen Hoover

Published: August 5, 2014
Genre: Romance
Pages: 322

Ugly Love is a book about overcoming, tragedy and romance by the American author Colleen Hoover and was first published by Atria Books[1] on August 5, 2014 and in Portugal, for the first time and by TopSeller, on June 8, 2015.

This book was nominated at the Goodreads Choice Awards in the category of Best Novel in 2014
