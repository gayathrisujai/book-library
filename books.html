It Ends with Us

“It Ends with Us” compassionately explores victim psychology and the courage required to forge a new path. The characters' complex bonds and motivations illustrate why there are no easy answers. This vivid tale ultimately champions prioritizing self-love and living authentically, even when it means letting go of long-held hopes and expectations.

It Starts With Us

It Starts With Us is a romance novel by Colleen Hoover. The novel follows the story of Lily Bloom, a single mother escaping an abusive marriage and reuniting with her first love, Atlas Corrigan, a chef who escaped his violent family as a teen3. The novel explores the complex and touching journey of Lily and Atlas as they navigate their past traumas, their present struggles, and their hopes for a shared future.

All Your Perfects

All Your Perfects by Colleen Hoover is a novel about a troubled marriage. The book explores the complexities of marriage, including infertility and infidelity. It offers a realistic portrayal of love after the “happily ever after” and emphasizes the importance of fighting for the relationships we cherish. The novel alternates between past joys and present struggles, leading to a heart-wrenching climax and a hopeful resolution as the couple rediscover love and start anew at a different place.

Ugly Love

“This Girl” is a poignant and emotional conclusion to the “Slammed” series. Colleen Hoover’s exquisite storytelling and vivid character development bring the love story of Layken and Will to life. The novel explores themes of love, forgiveness, and the power of communication in a relationship.

Heart Bones

"Heart Bones" by Colleen Hoover is a poignant novel that explores young love, class disparities, and personal trauma. The story follows Beyah Grim, a resilient young woman, and Samson as they navigate societal boundaries and share their darkest secrets. The book portrays how love can bridge disparate worlds, offering a chance for healing and redemption.

Finding Perfect

Finding Perfect is a short story by Colleen Hoover that connects the Hopeless series and All Your Perfects1. It follows Daniel, who is married to Six, as he tries to find out what is wrong with their relationship. He discovers a shocking truth that changes everything and makes him question his love and his humanity. The story explores the themes of motherhood, true love, and decency.
